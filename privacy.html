Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

GoldNova respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights. We keep it simple — we only collect what we need to run the service.

1. Who We Are

GoldNova is an automated gold trading software service. References to "we", "us", or "our" in this policy refer to GoldNova and its operators. For privacy enquiries, contact us at support@goldnova.io.

2. What Data We Collect

We collect only the minimum data necessary to operate the service:

  • Account data — your name and email address when you subscribe
  • Payment data — processed securely by Stripe. We never see or store your card details
  • Licence data — your licence key and activation status
  • Support data — any information you share when contacting us for help
  • Usage data — basic analytics such as page visits (no personal identifiers)

We do not collect your broker credentials, trading history, or account balance. The bot runs locally on your machine and connects directly to your broker.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to:

  • Process your subscription and manage your account
  • Deliver your licence key and software access
  • Send transactional emails (receipts, licence keys, renewal notices)
  • Respond to support requests
  • Improve the service based on aggregated, anonymised usage data

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data on the following legal bases under UK GDPR:

  • Contract — processing your subscription and delivering the service
  • Legitimate interests — improving the service and preventing fraud
  • Legal obligation — where required by law

5. Third Parties

We use a small number of trusted third-party services to operate GoldNova:

  • Stripe — payment processing. Subject to Stripe's own privacy policy
  • GitHub Pages — website hosting
  • Email provider — for transactional emails only

We do not share your data with any other third parties.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your subscription is active and for a reasonable period afterwards for legal and accounting purposes (typically 6 years in line with UK law). You may request earlier deletion — see your rights below.

7. Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies — only those necessary for the site to function. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. No cookie consent banner is required as we only use essential cookies.

8. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct any inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restrict or object to how we process your data
  • Data portability — receive your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent at any time (where processing is based on consent)

To exercise any of these rights, email us at support@goldnova.io. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data. Payments are handled entirely by Stripe and are never processed or stored on our systems. Your broker API keys are stored locally on your own machine and are never transmitted to us.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by email. The date at the top of this page reflects when it was last updated.

11. Contact & Complaints

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at support@goldnova.io.

If you are unhappy with how we handle your data, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.
